# Lumen SAS — Internal HR Policy (Confidential)

> Fictional company used for the workshop. This is private data the public model
> has never seen — that is the whole point of RAG.

## Paid leave (congés payés)

Full-time employees at Lumen accrue **2.5 working days of paid leave per month**,
totalling **30 working days per year**. The reference period runs from **1 June to
31 May**. Up to **10 unused days** may be carried over and must be used before
**30 September** of the following period, after which they are forfeited.

## Remote work

Lumen operates a **hybrid policy**: a maximum of **3 remote days per week**.
Fully remote arrangements require manager approval and are reviewed every 6 months.
Employees based outside France or Luxembourg must have an approved EU work location
for GDPR and tax-residency reasons.

## Sick leave

A medical certificate must be submitted within **48 hours** of the first day of
absence. The first **3 days** are subject to the statutory waiting period unless
the collective agreement states otherwise.

## Expense reimbursement

Business expenses are reimbursed within **30 days** of submission through the
internal portal. Meals during business travel are capped at **25 EUR per meal**.
Receipts are mandatory; expenses without a receipt are rejected.

## Equipment

Each engineer receives a laptop refreshed every **3 years**. Requests for
additional hardware go through the IT ticketing system and require manager sign-off
above **500 EUR**.
